Overview

We empower the future of education through innovative online initiatives, cutting-edge pedagogies, and state-of-the-art learning technologies. We need people who want to play a vital role in delivering high-quality academic programs and co-creating an exceptional digital learning experience for our students. We are excited about the impact our work can have on students' lives and seek individuals who share our passion for learning and growth. Here at SUSS VLI, we value the DevOps characteristics of adaptability and continuous improvement, encouraging our team to grow in unprecedented ways and challenge the boundaries of innovation. We foster a collaborative environment that values creativity and encourages everyone to contribute to impactful solutions. Mistakes are seen as valuable learning opportunities. Failure is embraced to promote progress and resilience. Join us, and help revolutionize the way students learn and grow in the digital age!

How We Work

At SUSS VLI, we are change-makers, innovating for impactful education. Speak up, deconstruct challenges, and co-create solutions with us. We embrace a culture of continuous learning and growth, providing vast opportunities to expand your horizons. Our team comprises technical experts, creating a collaborative environment where knowledge sharing is encouraged.

We prioritize creating a positive workspace, encouraging collaboration and innovation, fostering a healthy and supportive atmosphere. Embracing a vibrant and inclusive culture is an integral part of being a member of the SUSS VLI team.

We prioritize your well-being with flexible work arrangements and generous leave benefits, supporting a balanced work and family life. Embrace the freedom to work from home or engage in physical activities as needed. We believe that productivity thrives when you have the autonomy to choose where and how you work best. Trusting in your capabilities, we empower you to excel in your role, no matter where you are. Join our team and experience the benefits of a supportive and adaptable work environment – apply now!

What You Can Contribute

As an engineering intern at SUSS VLI, you'll embark on an exciting journey of impactful contributions. With hands-on experience in Full-Stack projects encompassing Python and JavaScript, you'll be at the forefront of developing cutting-edge solutions that power the future of education. Dive into the world of DevOps Pipeline and Infrastructure, where you'll optimize and streamline our processes, ensuring seamless delivery of educational initiatives. Additionally, explore Analytics and Data-related work, extracting valuable insights to enhance the learning experience for our students.

By joining SUSS VLI, you'll co-create innovative solutions and play a vital role in shaping the landscape of education, leaving a lasting impact on our students and the community. Embrace this opportunity to grow and thrive as a dynamic engineering intern, contributing to meaningful projects that make a difference.

Our Hiring Process

We are seeking individuals who share our excitement and passion for building the future of education. To find these exceptional candidates, we prioritize hiring right. This means focusing on identifying and selecting the most suitable individuals who not only possess the required technical abilities but also align with our company's values, goals, and work culture.

To prepare you for our hiring process, we want to provide you with some valuable tips and details on how to shine during the application process. By following these guidelines, you can ensure that we don't miss out on hiring the right person for our team. Your unique qualities and skills will play a vital role in shaping the future of education together at SUSS VLI.

Creating your CV

  • Ensure that your CV is simple and easy to read. Use a clean format.
  • Showcase any accomplishment or projects.
  • If you are confident with your craft, please feel free to state them in your CV.
  • If you have a GitHub or Hugging Face account, please include them in your CV.

Application and Interview Process

  1. Apply and submit your CV here
  2. Zoom Interview (30 - 45 minutes): We may ask you questions about your coding project.
  3. Optional: Take Home Coding Project (3 - 5 hours)

Eligibility Criteria and Qualifications

  • Pursuing or recently completed a degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Information Systems, or a related field.
  • Basic Technical Competencies (Python, JavaScript...)
  • Familiarity with Software Development Tools (e.g. Git, Visual Studio Code)
  • Thinking and Analytics Skills to problem solve.
  • Willingness to learn and make mistakes

If you are ready to join us, apply now. Good luck!
